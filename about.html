About Samantha

Samantha Ruth Prabhu is an acclaimed actor, producer, entrepreneur, and philanthropist, known for her versatility and magnetic screen presence. With a career spanning over a decade in the Indian film industry, she has redefined female-led narratives, delivering powerful performances across Tamil and Telugu cinema. From the charming Jessie in Ye Maaya Chesave to the fierce Raji in The Family Man 2, Samantha has effortlessly portrayed a spectrum of characters, earning her multiple Filmfare and SIIMA Awards. .

Beyond acting, Samantha is a passionate creator—an entrepreneur with her fashion label Saaki, a wellness advocate, and a philanthropist through her foundation, Pratyusha Support, which provides healthcare for women and children. She continues to push boundaries, exploring new mediums and storytelling formats, including digital platforms and production ventures. When she’s not on set, Samantha can be found indulging in her love for books, fitness, travel, and experimenting with fashion. She is an advocate for self-love, wellness, and personal growth, inspiring millions with her resilience and authenticity.
